About Me

My name is William Gannon - but you can call me Bill. I work for the University of Texas Libraries as Technical Staff. Currently, I am enrolled in the University of Texas Full Stack Coding Bootcamp. I am studying HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and look forward to learning much more in the coming months. Upon completion of the program I will be looking to start a Development career here in Austin.

I moved to Austin in 2012 with my wife, Cynthia. We previously lived in Boston, where we met while attending Brandeis University. There I double-majored in Economics and Philosophy. I am originally from Albany, New York and still have a lot of family there.

My hobbies include cycling, film, pub trivia, and travel. I recently visited Mexico City for the first time and look forward to returning soon.
